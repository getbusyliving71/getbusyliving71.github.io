Who We Are

Ramon and Keesha White are the owners of Get Busy Living-GBL Property Solutions, LLC. They have been married for over 15 years and have two children, Maliak and Myles. Ramon is a native of Atlanta and Keesha moved from Mississippi over 20 years ago. The couple have a love for the community, which fueled them to start a real estate investment business. Being involved and active in their local community has sparked their desire to expand community development on a larger scale. Their passion to improve and grow communities is very important, therefore, allowing them to not only give back but revitalize communities.

The business owners are committed to the company name of Get Busy Living. Ramon and Keesha’s philosophy is to serve everyone with professionalism, honesty and integrity. They love family activities, traveling, and volunteering.
